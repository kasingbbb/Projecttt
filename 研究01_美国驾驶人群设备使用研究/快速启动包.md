### 行动2：准备筛选流程（15分钟）

**快速筛选问题（私信回复时使用）：**

```
Hi! Thanks for your interest! Quick questions:

1. What specific change did you make?
   (e.g., "stopped using car nav, now use Google Maps")

2. When did you make this change?
   (e.g., "3 months ago after a bad experience")

3. Are you still using the new way?

4. Do you drive at least 3x/week?

If yes to all, you're exactly who I need! 🎉
```

**合格标准：**
- ✅ 能说出具体改变
- ✅ 6个月内
- ✅ 持续使用新方法
- ✅ 每周开车≥3次
